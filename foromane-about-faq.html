What Is Foromane

Foromane App is a brand-centric discovery platform and business directory designed specifically for the Botswana market. It serves as a "Digital Engine" connecting general users, tradespeople, and professionals with local businesses across 27 distinct industry categories.

For Users: It’s a tool to discover local suppliers, browse promotional catalogs, save items to personalized project notes, and calculate costs. For Businesses: It’s a platform to showcase their brand, list their contacts, promote products, and track profile views.

Foromane is built on a Hybrid Cloud-Local Architecture, meaning it downloads and caches data so it feels blazingly fast and works seamlessly even when your internet connection is poor or completely offline.

FAQs
Do I need an internet connection to use Foromane App?
You need an internet connection to sync the latest promos and business profiles. However, Foromane is built to be "Offline-First". Once you view an image or sync the platform assets using the "Sync Assets Now" button in your account, the app saves them locally. You can browse your saved notes and cached profiles without using mobile data!
How do I calculate the cost of my construction project?
Use the Notes feature! Browse the Promos feed, save the materials you need to a Note, and then open that Note. Adjust the quantities for each item, and Foromane will automatically calculate your total project cost.
How do I share my project budget with a client?
Open your compiled Note and tap the green WhatsApp share icon. Foromane will automatically generate a professional, itemized list with quantities, supplier names, and total costs, ready to send via WhatsApp.
I am a business owner. How do I get my business listed?
You can submit your business details (Name, Category, Town, GPS link, Social Media, Logo, and Banner) directly through the "Add Business" onboarding form in the app. Once submitted, it goes into a "Pending" queue. A Foromane Administrator will review your submission and approve it, at which point it will instantly appear live in the Directory.
Can I see how many people are viewing my business profile?
Yes! Foromane features Live KPI Tracking. Every time a user opens your business profile, your "Views" counter increments, allowing you to track your brand's reach on the platform.
How do I pay for Promos or more Notes?
Foromane supports local mobile money. You can select to pay via BTC Smega, Mascom Myzaka, or Orange Money. The app will provide you with the exact payment details and prompt you to send your proof of payment via WhatsApp for quick processing.
